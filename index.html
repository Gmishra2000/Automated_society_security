How it works ? Working our product

Our product setup is fully customisable and super easy to use. All the features and be remotely controlled and accessed.

  • 01 Quick Registration of Visitors

    The society needs to register the visitors with our system. We just need the visitors basic detail and picture of them . Thats it!! Now our product is ready to GO!

  • 02 Authorisation of Visitors

    All the visitors need to do a quick validation at society's gate. They just need to stand near our product and look in the camera. Our product wil do their temprature measurement and face recognition. If everything is fine then the vistors are allowed to enter society.

  • 03 End of day Report Generation

    All the visitors visting the society will be recorded. A detailed report of the whole day will be generated and will be mailed to the authority incharge automatically.
